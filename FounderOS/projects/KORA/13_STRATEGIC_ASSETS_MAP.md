# KORA LAB — 13. STRATEGIC ASSETS MAP

**Version:** 1.0

---

# Purpose

The Strategic Assets Map defines every strategic asset Kora Lab must accumulate to fulfill its mission.

The purpose of the company is not merely to build products.

Its purpose is to accumulate assets that make its mission increasingly inevitable.

Every project,

every investment,

every hire,

every partnership,

every research effort,

should strengthen one or more strategic assets.

If an initiative does not increase strategic assets,

its long-term value should be questioned.

---

# What Is A Strategic Asset?

A Strategic Asset is something that:

* becomes more valuable over time;
* is difficult to reproduce;
* increases long-term competitive advantage;
* strengthens sovereignty;
* compounds future capabilities.

Assets create leverage.

Projects consume resources.

The objective is to convert projects into assets.

# What Kora Should Never Optimize

Never maximize:

Short-term revenue

if it destroys

Research.

Never maximize:

Brand

if it weakens

Trust.

Never maximize:

Distribution

if model quality stagnates.

Never maximize:

Fundraising

Assets have hierarchy.

---

# Decision Filter

Before approving any major initiative:

1. Which strategic asset does this strengthen?
2. Is that asset compounding?
3. Is the asset difficult for competitors to reproduce?
4. Does it strengthen African sovereignty?
5. Will this asset still matter in ten years?

If no strategic asset improves,

the initiative is probably tactical,

not strategic.
