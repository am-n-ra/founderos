# EDENVALLEY — Strategy

## Vision

EdenValley is the innovation academy and incubator where KORA-enabled innovations become real ventures.

## Principles

- Literacy is not a barrier to innovation
- Frontier AI in every African language
- Every innovation guided to venture creation
- Change starts at the grassroots

## Ecosystem Position

1. **KORA Lab** — Develop frontier AI. End AI imports to Africa.
2. **OMNI** — Mapping/address infrastructure for Africa.
3. **EdenValley** — Incubator that turns KORA-enabled innovation into real ventures.

## Status

Concept stage. Requires KORA Track 2b operational before full launch.
