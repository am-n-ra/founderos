# EDENVALLEY

## Concept

EdenValley is the innovation academy and incubator of change for the African continent. It is the house that collects innovations enabled by KORA's frontier models and guides them toward venture creation.

## Core Identity

Even the most disadvantaged — those who cannot read, write, or speak a foreign language — can use the full power of KORA's frontier models. KORA's Track 2b (2000+ African languages, no literacy required) makes this possible. EdenValley catches the wave:

- People who know no code will build software using KORA's models (as capable as Claude/GPT frontier models)
- People who speak only French, Ewe, or any African language will innovate
- EdenValley collects, mentors, and incubates these innovations into ventures

## Position in FounderHQ Ecosystem

EdenValley is the third pillar:

1. **KORA Lab** — Develop frontier AI. End AI imports to Africa.
2. **OMNI** — Mapping/address infrastructure for Africa (equivalent to what Maps provides in developed countries).
3. **EdenValley** — The incubator that turns KORA-enabled innovation into real ventures. An academy of innovation and genius, not limited by literacy.

## Principles

- Literacy is not a barrier to innovation
- Frontier AI in every African language
- Every innovation guided to venture creation
- Change starts at the grassroots
