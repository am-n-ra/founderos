## Content Adaptation Guide

### TikTok / YouTube Shorts / FB Reels (9:16)
- Duration: 30-60s
- Text overlay: 3-5 words, large font
- Hook: first 3 seconds
- CTA: "Lien dans la bio"
- Music: subtle, tension-building

### LinkedIn (Text Post)
- Duration: 300-500 words
- Hook: first line must stop the scroll
- Structure: Hook → Data → Insight → CTA
- CTA: "Lien en commentaire"
- Tone: professional, authoritative

### Facebook Reels
- Same as TikTok
- Add: "Partagez avec un parent" as secondary CTA
