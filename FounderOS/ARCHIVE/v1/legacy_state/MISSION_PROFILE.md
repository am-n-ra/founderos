# MISSION PROFILE

## Mission Statement
Build African AI infrastructure and language ecosystems that serve African communities.

## Vision
A world where African languages are first-class citizens in AI, not afterthoughts.

## 1 Year Goal
KORA generates sustainable revenue, product validated with real users, FounderOS fully operational.

## 3 Year Goal
KORA is a recognized platform for African language AI with paying customers, team, and funding.

## 10 Year Goal
African AI infrastructure that reduces dependency on foreign AI providers for African languages.

## Lifetime Goal
African AI sovereignty through language infrastructure, education, and ecosystem building.

## Theory of Change
1. Build language datasets and tools (KORA)
2. Develop products that create immediate value
3. Generate revenue to fund deeper research
4. Expand to more languages and capabilities
5. Train next generation of African AI builders
