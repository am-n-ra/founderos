# Continuous Improvement Protocol

## Purpose
Prevent stagnation. A system that does not improve eventually becomes obsolete.

## Prime Directive
Every completed project triggers improvement review.

## Review Questions
What worked? What failed? What surprised us? What repeated? What should change?

## Review Frequency
Daily: completed tasks, blocked tasks, lessons, next priorities.
Weekly: progress, patterns emerging, workflow issues, opportunities.
Monthly: strategic alignment, system performance, knowledge growth, playbook growth.
Quarterly: major milestones, direction correction.

## Metrics
Lessons Count, Patterns Count, Playbooks Count, Workflow Success Rate, Execution Speed, Goal Completion Rate.
