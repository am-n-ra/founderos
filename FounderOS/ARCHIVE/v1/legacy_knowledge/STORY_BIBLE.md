# Story Bible

## Purpose
A video is temporary. A project universe is persistent. The Story Bible is the source of truth for all content belonging to a project, brand, campaign, documentary, channel, podcast or series.

## Scope
Brand, Campaign, Series, Podcast, Documentary, Channel, Product, Founder Journey, Educational Program.

## Structure
PROJECT_NAME, MISSION, AUDIENCE, CONTENT OBJECTIVE, VISUAL IDENTITY (color palette, lighting, camera style, typography), NARRATIVE IDENTITY (tone, pacing, emotion, narration style), BRAND IDENTITY (values, positioning, voice), RECURRING ENTITIES, RECURRING LOCATIONS, CONTENT RULES, FORBIDDEN RULES.

## Consistency Rule
All future content inherits: Story Bible → Entity Registry → Scenes → Assets.
