# Series Bible

## Purpose
A single video is an asset. A series is an ecosystem. The Series Bible maintains continuity across episodes.

## Structure
SERIES_NAME, MISSION, AUDIENCE, FORMAT (short form, long form, podcast, documentary, educational, founder journal, interview), NARRATIVE ARC (beginning, middle, end, major milestones), RECURRING ENTITIES, RECURRING LOCATIONS, VISUAL RULES, AUDIO RULES, CONTINUITY RULES, EPISODE TRACKER.

## Continuity Gate
Before new episode: verify consistency with previous episodes.

## Evolution Rule
Series can evolve. Changes must be recorded.

## Episode Tracker
EPISODE_ID, DATE, SUMMARY, KEY EVENTS, NEW ENTITIES, NEW LOCATIONS, LESSONS.
