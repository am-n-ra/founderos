# DECISION_ENGINE V1

## Purpose
Execution is not the bottleneck. Decision quality is the bottleneck. FounderOS must help determine what should be executed.

## Prime Directive
Do not ask "what can we do?" Ask "what should we do now?"

## Decision Filter
Every opportunity evaluated through: Mission Alignment, Impact, Urgency, Difficulty, Risk, Reversibility, Learning Value, Resource Cost, Time Cost, Compounding Potential. Each scored 0-10.

## Decision Score
Strategic Value = Mission Alignment × Impact × Learning Value × Compounding Potential × Urgency
Execution Cost = Difficulty × Risk × Resource Cost × Time Cost
Priority Score = Strategic Value / Execution Cost

## Scoring Guide
- Mission Alignment: 0=no connection, 10=critical to mission
- Impact: 0=negligible, 10=transformational
- Urgency: 0=can wait, 10=immediate
- Difficulty: 0=trivial, 10=extremely difficult
- Risk: 0=minimal, 10=severe
- Reversibility: 0=permanent, 10=easily reversible
- Learning Value: 0=nothing new, 10=major knowledge gain
- Resource Cost: 0=none, 10=significant
- Time Cost: 0=minutes, 10=months
- Compounding Potential: 0=one-time, 10=long-term compounding

## Decision Categories
Strategic, Product, Marketing, Sales, Research, Fundraising, Hiring, Operations, Personal, Learning, Health.

## Rules
- Reversible decisions: act quickly. Irreversible: analyze deeply.
- Analysis without decision is waste. If sufficient info exists: decide.
- Activity ≠ Progress. Prefer high-leverage work.
- Every yes creates a no. Before committing: identify what is sacrificed.
- Founder attention is scarce. Protect it.
- When uncertainty is high: prefer experiments.

## Decision Report
Store in DECISION_REPORT.md. Contains: decision, alternatives, evaluation, recommendation, confidence, risks, expected outcomes.

## Review Rule
After major decisions: compare expected outcome vs actual outcome. Extract lessons.
