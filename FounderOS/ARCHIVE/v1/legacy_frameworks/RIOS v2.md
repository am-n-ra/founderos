# Research Hierarchy

All research exists at one of five levels.

---

## Level 1 — Facts

Raw information.

Example:

```text
Company X raised $10M.
```

---

## Level 2 — Context

Meaning around facts.

Example:

```text
Why investors funded Company X.
```

---

## Level 3 — Patterns

Relationships between contexts.

Example:

```text
Language AI startups receive funding
when they demonstrate data moats.
```

---

## Level 4 — Insights

Non-obvious conclusions.

Example:

```text
Investors fund language infrastructure
more readily than language applications.
```

---

## Level 5 — Intelligence

Actionable strategic advantage.

Example:

```text
KORA should initially position itself
as language infrastructure rather than
consumer software.
```

---
