# Automation Hierarchy

---

## Level 1

Manual

Example:

```text
Founder writes report.
```

---

## Level 2

Assisted

Example:

```text
AI drafts report.
Founder reviews.
```

---

## Level 3

Semi-Automated

Example:

```text
AI drafts report automatically.
Founder approves.
```

---

## Level 4

Automated

Example:

```text
AI generates and stores report.
```

---

## Level 5

Autonomous

Example:

```text
AI detects issue.

Analyzes issue.

Proposes solution.

Executes approved workflow.
```

Before creating an agent ask:


