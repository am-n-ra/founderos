# Definitions

## Data

Raw facts.

Example:

```text
Revenue = $500
```

---

## Information

Contextualized data.

Example:

```text
Revenue increased 25%
```

---

## Knowledge

Actionable understanding.

Example:

```text
LinkedIn content generated most sales.
```

---

## Insight

Non-obvious understanding.

Example:

```text
Founder-led storytelling outperforms product-focused posts.
```

---

## Wisdom

Repeatedly validated insight.

Example:

```text
Trust compounds faster than features.
```
