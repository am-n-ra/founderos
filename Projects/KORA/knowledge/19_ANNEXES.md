# KORA LAB — 19. ANNEXES

**Version:** 1.0

---

# Purpose

The Annexes contain all supporting evidence required to validate the Business Plan.

They transform strategic claims into verifiable information.

The Business Plan answers:

**What is Kora?**

The Annexes answer:

**How do we know this is true?**

They are living documents that evolve continuously.

---

# Structure

The Annexes are organized into independent sections.

Each annex may evolve without requiring modification of the Business Plan.

# Governance

Every Annex has:

* an owner;
* a version number;
* a review date;
* a status;
* a source of truth.

No information should exist in multiple annexes.

Rule 0 (Single Source of Truth) applies.

---

# Maintenance Policy

The Annexes evolve continuously.

Unlike the Vision or Mission,

they are operational documents.

Updates should occur whenever new evidence,

metrics,

products,

or decisions emerge.

---

# Relationship With Other Documents

The Annexes support every document within the Venture Architecture.

They provide the evidence,

analysis,

data,

financial models,

technical documentation,

and institutional memory required to maintain credibility.

Together,

the 19 VAOS documents form a complete venture architecture capable of transforming an initial idea into an institution.
