## Strategic Ideas

### Voice-First AI Platform
- Native voice interface for non-literate users
- Could become KORA's primary differentiator vs global frontier labs
- Same models, two interfaces (text + voice)

### Cross-Lingual Native Models
- User speaks in Ewe → response in Ewe, or document generated in English/Chinese
- No translation pipeline. Native capability of the model.
- Potential competitive moat against labs that treat African languages as an afterthought

### Multi-Account Kaggle Strategy
- 10 accounts = 300h/week of T4x2 GPU time
- Free, immediately available
- Could train first 300-500M MoE model in 1-2 weeks
- Force multiplier at zero cost

### Language Data as Strategic Asset
- Ewe data collection pipeline could become repeatable for 2000+ languages
- Each language dataset is a compounding, non-replicable asset
- Could be the moat that protects KORA from large AI companies expanding into African languages

### Fixed Devices for Remote Institutions
