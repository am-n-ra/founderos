**Version:** 1.0

# Executive Summary

Kora Lab is an African Frontier AI laboratory whose mission is to eliminate Africa's dependence on imported Artificial Intelligence.

Rather than adapting foreign AI systems,

Kora develops sovereign frontier AI models capable of competing with the world's leading laboratories while being designed specifically for African realities.

The objective is not merely technological independence.

The objective is to create the scientific, industrial, and economic capabilities that allow Africa to participate as a creator during every future technological revolution.

# Vision

Instead,

Africa develops,

# Mission

Develop sovereign frontier AI models and AI infrastructure that eliminate technological dependence while making advanced AI universally accessible across Africa.

---

# Problem

Today,

Africa primarily consumes AI developed elsewhere.

This creates multiple structural dependencies:

* technological dependence;
* linguistic exclusion;
* economic dependence;
* scientific dependence;
* strategic vulnerability.

Even where AI exists,

millions remain excluded because the interfaces assume literacy,

internet quality,

or languages that many Africans do not use.

Artificial Intelligence is becoming foundational infrastructure.
