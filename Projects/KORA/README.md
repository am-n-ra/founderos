# KORA — Knowledge & Oral Representation for Africa

**Status:** Pre-Seed / Validation | **Phase:** Phase I — Survival

## Mission

Design, train, deploy and continuously improve sovereign world-class artificial intelligence systems that eliminate Africa's dependence on imported AI while making frontier intelligence accessible to every African, regardless of language, literacy or socioeconomic condition.

## Vision

Africa no longer imports the artificial intelligence it depends on. Instead, Africa designs, trains, owns, deploys, and continuously improves world-class AI systems that compete globally while remaining universally accessible across the continent.
