## 5. MONTHLY/YEARLY — Special Periods

### Sankranti (Sun Transitions)
- **Makar Sankranti** (Jan 14 — Sun enters Capricorn): Starts Uttarayana (6-month auspicious period). Best time all year for business launches, registrations, major commitments. **Priority: HIGH**
- **Sun exaltation** (Mesha/Aries, ~Apr 14): Peak vitality month. Best for career moves requiring confidence. **Priority: HIGH**
- **Sun debilitation** (Tula/Libra, ~Oct 17): Avoid ego-driven decisions. Focus on diplomacy and partnership. **Priority: MEDIUM**
- **General Sankranti rule**: Avoid starting major initiatives 2 hours before/after any Sun sign change. Sensitive transition period.

### Eclipse Cycles (Grahan)
- **Avoid ALL new ventures** during eclipse window (day of) and 1-2 weeks before/after each eclipse
- Solar eclipse (Surya Grahan): Sutak begins 12 hours before — no new beginnings
- Lunar eclipse (Chandra Grahan): Sutak begins 9 hours before — no new beginnings
- Eclipse season (between paired eclipses): Avoid major karmic initiations
- Use eclipse periods for: review, completion, spiritual practice, strategy refinement
- **Priority: HIGH** — This is the strongest "avoid" signal in Vedic timing

### Mercury Retrograde (Budha Vakri) — ~3x/year, 21-24 days each
- **Vedic perspective differs from pop astrology**: Retrograde Mercury is considered STRONGER (not weaker) — intensified but unpredictable
- **DO**: Revise, review, reconnect with old contacts, fix bugs, complete unfinished work, strategy refinement, deep analysis
- **AVOID**: Signing NEW contracts, launching products, starting NEW ventures, making irreversible commitments
- **WHY**: Mercury governs contracts and communication. "Re-do" energy favors correction over creation
- **Priority: HIGH**

### Kharmas (Sun in Sagittarius, ~Dec 16 – Jan 14)
- Avoid major new ventures, marriages, celebrations
- Energy is "low vitality" — focus on planning and preparation
- Ends at Makar Sankranti
- **Priority: MEDIUM**

### Pitru Paksha (Ancestral fortnight, ~Sep/Oct)
- No new ventures, launches, or celebrations
- Focus on honoring ancestors, completion, reflection
- **Priority: LOW** (only for those who observe)

### Akshaya Tritiya (~May)
- Single most auspicious day for starting anything new
- No muhurta checking needed — universally beneficial
- **Priority: HIGH** (mark on calendar as #1 launch day)

2. **Mercury Hora** for ALL communication work (most productive daily window)
3. **Jupiter Hora** for ALL financial/strategic decisions
4. **Wednesday** = peak communication/commerce day. **Thursday** = peak growth/strategy day
5. **Shukla Paksha** (waxing moon, ~2 weeks/month) = the only window for new beginnings
6. **Avoid: Eclipse periods, Mercury retrograde new launches, Rahu Kaal, Amavasya new ventures**
7. **Makar Sankranti** (Jan 14) opens the 6-month Uttarayana window — best stretch of year for launching
8. **Color of the day** + **Hora of the activity** + **Nakshatra of the day** layered together = maximum precision

### HARD AVOIDS (Non-negotiable):
- ❌ Launching during eclipse (Sutak period)
- ❌ Signing contracts during Mercury retrograde
- ❌ Starting new ventures during Amavasya (New Moon)
- ❌ Important decisions during Rahu Kaal (daily 90-min inauspicious window)
- ❌ Ego-driven decisions during Sun debilitation (Libra transit, Oct-Nov)
