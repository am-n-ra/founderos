# RESEARCH PROTOCOL
## When to Research
- New competitor/partner identified
- Grant or funding call discovered
- Technical question blocking progress
- Market validation needed

## Research Depth Levels
1. Quick scan (5 min web search)
2. Deep dive (30 min + analysis)
3. Watchtower registration (ongoing automated)

## Output
- Level 1: Verbal summary
- Level 2: KNOWLEDGE.md entry
- Level 3: WATCH_REGISTRY.md entry
