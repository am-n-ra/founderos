# FounderOS V4 — KORA (Knowledge & Oral Representation for Africa)

## Core Vision (2026-06-27)

**KORA = Plus d'importation d'IA en Afrique.**

If KORA succeeds, Africa will no longer import the AI used within it. Simple, complete, complex.

KORA must deliver frontier-class models across ALL domains — code, text, image, video, embedding, audio — matching or exceeding what international players offer. At that quality level, Africa naturally stops importing AI. And because KORA becomes the African reference with continental market presence, it is globally competitive by default.

Beyond AI: KORA establishes the pattern so that Africa stops importing technology during future revolutions. Starting with the AI revolution, Africa catches up on all past delays and positions itself for every subsequent technological shift — space, biotech, quantum, whatever comes next. KORA is the template for technology sovereignty across all future revolutions.

The founder's life mission drives this: to be the man who allowed Africa to catch up. His deep why is intolerance for injustice and discrimination. Like Elon Musk whose every venture serves "make humanity interplanetary," every venture here serves "Africa at the same level as other continents."

## Two Steps

### Step 1: Globally Competitive Models
Build AI models as competent as the world's best across all domains (code, text, image, video, embedding, audio). Compete globally — not just "good enough for Africa."

### Step 2: Adapt for African Constraints
Once models are world-class, adapt them for:
- 2000+ languages (mostly oral)
- Intermittent/no connectivity, unstable power
- Modest hardware, shared devices
- Non-literate users
- Local currency payment, no international card

Step 2a (Power Users): those already educated can use models directly.
Step 2b (Everyone else): 2000+ languages, no literacy required.
