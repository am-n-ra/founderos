# EDENVALLEY

## Concept

EdenValley is the innovation academy and incubator of change for the African continent. It is the house that collects innovations enabled by KORA's frontier models and guides them toward venture creation.

## The Wave of Innovation

KORA's Track 2b makes frontier AI accessible in 2000+ African languages with no literacy required. This unlocks a wave of innovation from unexpected people — those who have never been able to participate in the technology revolution.

- People who know no code will build software using KORA's frontier models (as capable as Claude/GPT frontier models), just by speaking their language.
- People who speak only French, Ewe, Yoruba, or any African language will innovate without learning English or a programming language.
- EdenValley catches this wave: it collects, mentors, and incubates these innovations into ventures.

## Core Identity

Even the most disadvantaged — those who cannot read, write, or speak a foreign language — can use the full power of KORA's frontier models. KORA's Track 2b (2000+ African languages, no literacy required) makes this possible:

- KORA removes the barrier of literacy and foreign language
- EdenValley removes the barrier of business creation
- Together they form a pipeline: frontier AI access → innovation → venture creation

## Position in FounderHQ Ecosystem

EdenValley is the third pillar:

1. **KORA Lab** — Develop frontier AI. End AI imports to Africa.
2. **OMNI** — Mapping/address infrastructure for Africa (equivalent of Maps/Po Box in developed countries).
3. **EdenValley** — The incubator that turns KORA-enabled innovation into real ventures. An academy of innovation and genius, not limited by literacy.

KORA enables → EdenValley incubates → OMNI provides the infrastructure canvas. Every venture serves the same mission: Africa catches up.

## Principles

- Literacy is not a barrier to innovation
- Frontier AI in every African language
- Une vague d'innovations venues de partout — même de ceux qui n'ont jamais codé
- Every innovation guided to venture creation
- Change starts at the grassroots
