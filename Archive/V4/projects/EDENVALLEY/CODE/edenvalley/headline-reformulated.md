# Original Reformulated

Original: "Eden Valley is the world's first startup incubator built for ADHD founders — and the people who build, back, and fund them."

---

## Option A - Deux phrases courtes

> Eden Valley is the world's first startup incubator for ADHD founders.
> And the people who build, back, and fund them.

(Rythme: pose + punchline)

---

## Option B - "Where" pour connecter

> Eden Valley is the world's first startup incubator **where** ADHD founders meet the people who build, back, and fund them.

(Plus fluide, une seule phrase)

---

## Option C - Structure parallèle

> Eden Valley: the world's first incubator **for** ADHD founders, **by** the people who build, back, and fund them.

(Parallèle for/by, plus punchy)

---

## Option D - Version active

> The world's first startup incubator for ADHD founders—**built by** the people who build, back, and fund them.

("Built by" = créé par cette communauté)

---

## Option E - Très concise

> The world's first startup incubator for ADHD founders and their builders, backers, and funders.

(Noms directement, plus court)

---

## Option F - Avec émotion

> The world's first startup incubator where ADHD founders find the people who build, back, and believe in them.

("Believe in" plus fort que "fund")

---

## Option G - Double ligne optimale

> The world's first incubator for ADHD founders.
> **Built with** the people who build, back, and fund them.

("Built with" = plus naturel que "and")
