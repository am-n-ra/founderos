# Vision: Eden Valley - The New Incubator

## Locked Mission & Goal

**Mission:** "We close the Thinker-Doer gap — from ideation to execution."

**Goal:** "Enable Thinkers and Doers to build successful startups together by making both the idea AND the execution equally visible and valued."

---

## What is Eden Valley?

**One-Sentence Description:**
> "An AI-powered incubator where Thinkers and Doers with shared Passion build together, crowdfunders support early, and investors find deals."

---

## The Problem

### Thinkers
- Have brilliant ideas but can't execute
- World rewards execution, not vision
- Identity shame: "I'm just a dreamer"

### Doers
- Execute powerfully but on wrong foundations (their own or others')
- Treated as **labor**, not partners
- Blamed for decisions they never made
- No agency in what they build

### Together
- **65% of startups fail due to co-founder conflict**
- Without framework, even complementary pairs destroy each other

### Crowdfunders
- Want to support ideas early but no access
- Can't find promising teams

### Funders
- Want to invest but no reliable pipeline
- Can't evaluate teams easily

---

## The Insight

> **The problem is NOT finding each other. The problem is SURVIVING the relationship.**

Current platforms: **Matching only**
What Eden Valley does: **Matching + Infrastructure + Funding**

---

## Locked Moat

**((Thinker + Doer + Passion) + AI + Platform Structure)**

- Thinker/Doer: Roles (ideation ↔ execution)
- Passion: Shared purpose/industry matching
- AI Pischon: Specialized AI agents at every layer
- Platform Structure: Euphrates + Havila + all tools

---

## Platform Architecture

### Euphrates (Team Building Wall)

| Stage | What Happens | Pischon Helps With |
|-------|------------|--------------------|
| 1. Thinker has idea | Creates blueprint (mission/goal/vision) | Structure mission/goal/vision/architecture |
| 2. Posts blueprint | Visible to Doers | - |
| 3. Doers apply | Show "how I'll contribute" | Questions to ask, filter |
| 4. Thinker selects | Filters and chooses | AI interview help |
| 5. Team forms | Contracts, equity, roles | Generate agreements |
| 6. Build | Daily execution | Clarity when Thinker unavailable |

### Havila (Funding Wall)

| Stage | Who Sees | Funding Type |
|-------|---------|-------------|
| Pre-incorporation | Crowdfunders | Product/pre-order |
| Post-incorporation | VCs + Angels | Equity |

### Platform Features

- Blueprint protection (permissions + IP agreement)
- Integrated tools (GitHub, task management)
- Team management dashboard
- Funding connections

---

## What is Pischon?

**AI-powered assistant** with specialized agents:

- Execution help (KPIs, tasks)
- Pitch preparation
- Funder filtering

## Equity Structure (Locked)

| What | When | Amount |
|------|------|--------|
| **Our equity** | At incorporation | 5% |
| **1% of funding** | When funded (any source) | 1% |
| **Crowdfunders pool** | Only if venture succeeds | 20% of our 5% |
| **If venture fails** | No funding | Pool stays with us |

## User Flow

```
Thinker has idea → Creates blueprint (Pischon helps) → Posts on Euphrates
        ↓
Doers see and apply → Thinker selects team → Team forms
        ↓
Team posts on Havila → Crowdfunders support (pre-incorporation)
        ↓
(If) Need VC/Angels → We help incorporate → Show to VCs/Angels
        ↓
Funded → We take 1% + 5% → Crowdfunders get pool
```
