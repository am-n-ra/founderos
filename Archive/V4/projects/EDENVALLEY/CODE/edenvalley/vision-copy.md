#### What is ADHD? (and why does it matter here)
ADHD (Attention Deficit Hyperactivity Disorder) is a neurological difference — not a character flaw. People with ADHD are 6x more likely to become entrepreneurs than the general population. They generate breakthrough ideas, see patterns others miss, and hyperfocus on problems that fascinate them. But they struggle to start tasks, maintain structure, and execute alone. Eden Valley was built around that reality — not despite it.
