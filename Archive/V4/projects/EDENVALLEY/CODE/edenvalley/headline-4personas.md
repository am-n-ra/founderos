# Headline Options - Les 4 Personas

Personas: Thinker (vision), Doer (build), Backer (crowdfund), Investor (fund)

---

## Option A - 4 mots pour 4 personas

> The world's first incubator for ADHD founders.
> **Visionaries. Builders. Backers. Investors.**

(Chaque persona se reconnait dans un mot)

---

## Option B - 2 lignes, 2 camps

> The world's first incubator for ADHD founders.
> **Those who imagine. Those who build. Those who fund.**

(Thinkers / Doers / Backers+Investors)

---

## Option C - Action commune

> The world's first incubator for ADHD founders.
> **Where vision meets execution—and the world funds both.**

(Thinker = vision, Doer = execution, Backer/Investor = fund)

---

## Option D - "Founders" inclus les 4

> The world's first incubator for **founders with ADHD**.
> **And everyone who builds, backs, and funds them.**

("Founders" = Thinker+Doer, "builds" = Doer, "backs" = Backer, "funds" = Investor)

---

## Option E - Très inclusif

> The world's first incubator where
> **ADHD founders meet the people who build, back, and fund them.**

(Une seule phrase, tous inclus)

---

## Option F - Simple et direct

> The world's first incubator for **ADHD entrepreneurs and their ecosystem**.

("Entrepreneurs" = Thinker+Doer, "ecosystem" = Backer+Investor)

---

## Option G - La toute dernière version raccourcie

> The first incubator for **ADHD founders—and everyone who believes in them**.

(Founders = 2 personas, "everyone who believes" = tous les funders)

---

## Préférence?

- **A** pour la reconnaissance immédiate des 4
- **B/C** pour la poésie/rythme
- **D** pour garder l'esprit original mais mieux inclus
- **E** pour une phrase complète complète
- **F** pour la simplicité
- **G** pour le côté émotionnel "believes"
