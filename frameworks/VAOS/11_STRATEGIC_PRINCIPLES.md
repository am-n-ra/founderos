# VAOS — 11 STRATEGIC PRINCIPLES

## Purpose

The non-negotiable rules that guide every decision.

## Principle 1: World-class or nothing

African products must compete with the best in the world. Not "good enough for Africa" — good enough for anyone.

## Principle 2: Train, don't fine-tune

Fine-tuning produces sovereignty. It adapts a model controlled by another lab. Training from scratch is the only path to true sovereignty.

## Principle 3: Two interfaces, same models

Text for the educated (global standard). Voice for inclusion (African advantage). Same excellence on both. The API and the voice interface share the same backend.

## Principle 4: Revenue is oxygen, not mission

Without revenue, we die. But revenue serves the mission, not the reverse. API revenue from enterprises funds free access for excluded populations.

## Principle 5: Open where possible, sovereign where necessary

Publish research, benchmarks, small models. Keep strategy on frontier models. What is published must not compromise sovereignty.

## Principle 6: Long-term compounding

Every project must strengthen future projects through knowledge, infrastructure, datasets, talent, capital, and reputation.

## Decision Protocol

1. Check the 6 principles. If violated, answer is no.
2. If principles are silent, use the Doctrine (Doc 01).
3. Final filter: "Does this accelerate Africa's catch-up?"
