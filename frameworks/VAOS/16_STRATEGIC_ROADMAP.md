# VAOS — 16 STRATEGIC ROADMAP

## Purpose

Technical, product, and research roadmap.

## Year 1 (2026-2027): Proof

- Q3 2026: First KORA model trained (300-500M). HAIDI submitted. Lab announced.
- Q4 2026: Public text API. Voice interface POC. 100 users.
- Q1 2027: Second model (1B+). ST Digital confirmed. Revenue > $0.
- Q2 2027: OMNI integration. EDEN VALLEY starts.
- **Capital needed:** $38K (HAIDI) or stable EA Trading
- **Team:** Solo founder + AI copilot

## Year 2 (2027-2028): Scale

- 7B+ model trained from scratch
- 1,000+ monthly active users
- Distribution partners (telcos, institutions)
- **Capital needed:** $100-500K (seed)
- **Team:** 3-5 people

## Year 3 (2028-2029): Frontier

- Multimodal model (text + image + audio)
- 10,000+ users
- Hardware partners (wearables)
- **Capital needed:** $1-5M (Series A)
- **Team:** 10-20 people

## Year 4-5 (2029-2030): Ecosystem

- All model categories
- OMNI and EDEN VALLEY active
- African compute (datacenter)
- **Capital needed:** $10M+ (Series B)

## Big Gates

| Gate | Trigger | Decision |
|------|---------|----------|
| Gate 1 | First model doesn't hold comparison | Pivot category or shut down |
| Gate 2 | Cash < 0 before revenue | Absolute revenue priority or stop |
| Gate 3 | < 100 users at 12 months | Rethink distribution or shut down |
