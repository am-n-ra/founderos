# VAOS — 12 NORTH STAR

## Purpose

The ultimate metric. The variable that represents progress.

## The Metric

**North Star:** Percentage of AI usage in Africa covered by models built by KORA (or the venture's ecosystem).

## Why This Metric

This measures the reduction of dependency. Not just adoption. Progress is when Africa uses less imported AI and more locally-built AI.

## What It Predicts

If this percentage rises:
- Models are good enough (otherwise people wouldn't switch)
- Distribution is working (otherwise people couldn't access)
- The mission advances (less dependency)

## What It Does NOT Measure

- Revenue (important but not the north)
- Raw benchmarks (useful but intermediate)
- Papers published (important but not the north)

## How to Move It

- Train better models → organic switching
- Voice interfaces in African languages → new users
- Distribution via smartphones → reach
- Affordable API → developer adoption
- Institutional partnerships → rural access

## Annual Target (KORA)

**12 months:** 10,000 monthly active users (50% voice, 50% text).
