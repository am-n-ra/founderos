- Remote-first (Lome hub)
- Open source contributors welcome
- Decisions: founder garde la vision, equipe execute

## Key Decisions

- **Founder garde:** Vision, mission, qualite des modeles, partenaires cles, fundraising

- **Collectif:** Architecture decisions, technology stack
